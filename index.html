The internet has fundamentally changed the way human beings interact at an interpersonal level. Love was once found in the wild; at a grocery store, on the bus, or by meeting your friend’s boyfriend’s best friend at a dinner party. In the dawn of the age of the internet, we had anonymous forums posting personal ads detailing missed connections with beautiful strangers in passing or unrequited loves. Now we are resolved to the instant dopamine and game-like interface of dating apps. Long gone are the days of handwritten love letters shoved in lockers.. unless…
